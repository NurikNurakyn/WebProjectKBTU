About Mount Up

Community for verified mountain progress.

Mount Up is a frontend platform where clients can store mountain cards, track climber progress, and manage rank progression. Backend connection will be added later without redesigning the interface.

How It Works

  • 1. User creates account and receives rank Novice.
  • 2. Client or admin confirms completed climbs in profile.
  • 3. Rank increases automatically based on confirmed mountains.
  • 4. Each mountain page shows required rank, route tips and photos.

Navigation

  • Guide: short mountain cards and quick access to mountain wiki pages.
  • Profile: rank, level and visited mountains (frontend placeholders).
  • Auth pages: login and registration UI ready for backend integration.
